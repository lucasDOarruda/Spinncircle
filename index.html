What is Hula Hoop Dance?

Yes, there is more you can do than just spinning it around your waste! Hula hooping is a creative outlet that can open you up to a whole new way of moving. There are so many ways to move the hoop both on and off your body. Dancing with a prop is a great way to open new movement pathways and can be really great for people who are looking to build their confidence with movement. Hula hooping is also a great entry into the flow arts community and meeting new people!

What can I expect at a Spin Circle Class?

Classes are always relaxed and fun, there is no pressure to nail a trick right there and then. Your teacher will always have different ways to make your move a little easier or harder depending on your level. Classes are also designed so that you can come to any class without having attended before and have a great time. Most weeks we will be learning a range of moves and how to put them together to start to grow your own flow. Some weeks we will learn a little routine to help bring it all together.
